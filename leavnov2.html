Seafret

A British music duo --- singer Jack Sedman, guitarist Harry Draper. They have a debut studio album named Tell me It's Real which composed of singles such as Atlantis , Be There and Wildfire which I personally really fond of. Their songs either makes me wanna swim in the sea till the horizon or love someone until the end of the time. Moreover, One thing I really am amazed of is every songs are written by their own, some might have collaboration with other talented writers.

Daughter

Daughter Band

Daughter is an English indie folk trio--- singer Elena Tonra, guitarist Igor and drummer Remi. They have a total of two albums, If You Leave and Not to Disappear . Personally suggesting Don't Care , Youth , Run and Smother . Their songs mostly are inspirational because they depicted life in such a sophisticated way.
